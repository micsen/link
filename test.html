How to Hold a Microphone Like a Pro

The Confident Grip

Hold the microphone firmly but comfortably, close to your mouth for clear vocals.

Tip: Avoid covering the grille to maintain sound quality.

The Relaxed Performer

Lightly hold the microphone at a slight angle for a natural and stylish look.

Tip: Keep the mic steady to avoid feedback.

The Dynamic Performer

Switch hands smoothly or use both hands for emotional songs.

Tip: Practice switching hands without making noise.
